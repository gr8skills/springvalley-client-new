Welcome to Spring Valley Schools

Spring Valley School recognises the importance of working together and pride ourselves on the positive partnerships that are created between children, parents and ourselves. Collaborative learning runs through all of our work and our curriculum encourages innovation, problem solving and creative thinking. Our size enables us to offer unique learning experiences and a real drive to ensure that all children are given the very best of learning opportunities.
